Puppy Snap


Introduction to Puppy Snap:

Are you always confused and wondering what is the breed of the dog you met randomly on the street?

Are you always curious what is the breed of the dog your neighbor owns that is forever energetic?

Worry no more! With PuppySnap introduced by MYpets, you can easily know the breed of the dog by just simply upload the image of the dog following the instructions below!

Let's start to have some fun by uploading the picture of the dog you are interested in below!



Instruction:

1. Choose an image from gallery or take a photo

2. Press 'Find Breed'

3. Voila! The breed will be reveal shortly
